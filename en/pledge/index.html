On Digital Publishing

Jakob Jochmann

Taking Flight

A Digital Publishing Pledge

We believe digital publishing should be empowering. It should lower the barrier to entry and raise the bar of the thinkable. It should be beautiful.

We want to elevate books from their physical limitations. Treat texts with the respect they deserve in digital form. Set them free from the shackles of closed ecosystems: Like minds and parachutes, books work best when they are open.

Open has special meaning in the software domain; our framework will be Open Source. We want our tools to be free for everyone to publish their own works, for an ever more exciting world of reading.

We strive to radically deconstruct the book and capture its essence. We want to awaken the silicone heart beating behind the glass to give texts on screens a tangible quality. Make them touch us when we touch them.

No more dysfunctional facsimiles of print books. Aspire to greatness, instead of “good enough.” We want to create digital reading experiences to fall in love with!

We want to see a world where books compete on user experience. So we built it.

If you love books and want to see what they could become on screens, watch this space. We will gladly share our ideas—and we will ask for your input and your help to come up with even better ideas and make those ideas come true.
