On Digital Publishing

You found pxcraft.pub — yay!

We at pixelcraftbooks were disappointed in the state of digital literature. We set out to change reading experiences for the better. Well, today we are proud to finally present the first result of our efforts. A free and open digital publishing framework. You can think of it as a printing press for agnostic pixels. All you need to do is put your content in a box and crank the lever: The machine turns it into beautiful, responsive HTML with book-functionality and design.

Let's empower digital books to be their own platform: bake reading software into each book instead of writing books for proprietary e-book readers.

The pxcraft.pub machine transforms plain texts into beautiful digital reading experiences. Relying heavily on open source tools, the machine itself is free to use and free to customize to your needs. It was created to liberate content and to free the minds of creators from the mundane worries of publishing. It is designed to give creators the freedom to focus on their craft and imbue texts with awe.

We at pixelcraftbooks designed the pxcraft.pub machine for us. We wanted a machine that scales, is platform agnostic and lets us create the kinds of custom reading experiences we want to see in the world. We give it to you, because we believe the world deserves better reading experiences. We believe you deserve to be empowered to create them. And we believe we are publishers, not software developers, and we want to spend our time producing content, not ecosystems.

How does it work?

We would love your help to improve the machine.

need help putting it together? Well, we would like some help financing experiments with digital literature, so we're willing to help you, if you help us. Yes, we monetize the machine through services. But it's really just the means to an end: Experiments cost money. If we can subsidize our publishing efforts, we can pay all sorts of talented people to invent experiences that will blow your mind.
